Our Heritage

Three Decades of Discovery

Founded in 1996 by Dr. Arthur Vance, Ancient Earth began as a small independent laboratory processing specimens for European museums. Today, we are the premier private dealership for museum-grade geological artifacts.

We do not simply buy and sell; we fund excavations, oversee ethical extraction, and meticulously prepare each specimen in our state-of-the-art facilities. Our clients include major natural history museums, royalty, and discerning private collectors.
