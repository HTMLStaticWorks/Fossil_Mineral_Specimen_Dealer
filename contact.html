Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we handle all export permits, secure crating, and insured international logistics for museum-grade specimens.

Can I request an appraisal for my own specimen?

We offer professional scientific appraisals for high-tier specimens. Please contact us via the form above to discuss requirements.

How do you verify the authenticity of fossils?

Every specimen undergoes CT scanning and osteological analysis. We provide a signed Certificate of Authenticity and a full provenance report with every purchase.

Can I visit the gallery without an appointment?

To maintain the highest level of security and provide a personalized experience for our collectors, we are open by appointment only.
