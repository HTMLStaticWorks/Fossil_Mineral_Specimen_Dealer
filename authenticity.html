Uncompromising Authenticity

Every specimen is subjected to rigorous scientific evaluation and backed by a lifetime guarantee of authenticity.

1

Field Provenance

Documentation begins at the excavation site. We record GPS coordinates, stratigraphic layers, and photographic evidence of the piece in situ.

2

UV & X-Ray Analysis

In the lab, specimens undergo long and short wave UV inspection to detect any composites, glues, or undocumented restoration.

3

Certification

A detailed physical and digital certificate is issued, signed by our lead paleontologist, detailing the exact percentage of restoration (if any).
